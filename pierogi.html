Pierogi

This recipe features a savory potato and cheese filling that's wrapped in a soft and tender dough, and it's the perfect comfort food to enjoy on a cold winter's night.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sour cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the flour and salt. Add the egg and water and stir until a soft dough forms.
  2. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-10 minutes until it is smooth and elastic.
  3. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  4. In a separate pan, sauté the diced onion until translucent.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix together the mashed potatoes, grated cheddar cheese, and sautéed onions. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Roll out the dough on a floured surface and cut out circles using a biscuit cutter or glass.
  7. Spoon a small amount of the potato and cheese mixture onto each circle of dough.
  8. Fold the dough in half and pinch the edges to seal.
  9. Boil the pierogi in salted water for 3-4 minutes until they float to the surface.
  10. Serve the pierogi hot with a dollop of sour cream (optional).
