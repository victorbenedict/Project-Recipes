Honey Nuggets

Our recipe uses honey, peanut butter, and a variety of other ingredients to create a delicious and portable snack that's great for on-the-go.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (such as almonds or peanuts)
  • 1/2 cup dried fruit (such as raisins or cranberries)
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the honey and peanut butter until smooth.
  2. Add the rolled oats, chopped nuts, dried fruit, and shredded coconut (if using) to the bowl and stir to combine.
  3. Scoop the mixture by the tablespoonful and roll it into small balls or nuggets.
  4. Place the nuggets on a parchment-lined baking sheet and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to set.
  5. Once the nuggets are firm, transfer them to an airtight container and store in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
